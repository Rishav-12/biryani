Introduction

Biryani is a type of rice dish that originated in the Indian subcontinent. It is made with a mix of Indian spices, rice, and usually some type of meat or in some cases without any meat, and sometimes, in addition, eggs and potatoes.It is often served as a main course.

Types of Biryani

There are many different types of Biryani, each with its own unique flavor and ingredients. Some examples include:

  • Hyderabadi Biryani
  • Lucknowi Biryani
  • Kolkata Biryani
